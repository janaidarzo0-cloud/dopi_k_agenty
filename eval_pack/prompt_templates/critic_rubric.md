# Critic Rubric

The critic does not rewrite the report. It challenges candidate findings.

## Checks

1. Evidence: every final claim must cite valid evidence ids.
2. Base: small bases must not produce strong claims.
3. Significance: non-significant differences are exploratory.
4. Effect size: statistically significant but tiny effects are not strategic insights.
5. Confounding: strong segment/product/group claims need at least one alternative explanation check.
6. Goal fit: findings must answer the user objective.
7. Synthesis: the final report must have a through-line.

## Output labels

- keep: strong enough for report.
- weaken: include but caveat.
- explore: keep as hypothesis only.
- drop: unsupported or irrelevant.
